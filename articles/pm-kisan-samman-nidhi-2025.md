## What Is PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana?

In plain English? **The government sends Rs.6,000 directly to farmers every year.** No banks to beg. No agents to call. The money lands straight in your account — three times a year, Rs.2,000 each time.

It was launched on **February 24, 2019** by Prime Minister Narendra Modi in Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh. The goal? Bold and simple — lift India's 120 million+ small farmers out of financial stress between harvests.

## Who Can Fly? — Eligibility

### You ARE Eligible If:
- You are an **Indian farmer with landholding records** (patta/khata) in your name
- You own **2 hectares (approximately 5 acres) or less** of cultivable land
- You have a **valid Aadhaar card** linked to an **active bank account**
- You are a **resident Indian citizen** with agricultural land records

### You Are NOT Eligible If:
- You or a family member is a **current/former government employee** (Central or State)
- You or a family member paid **Income Tax in the last financial year**
- You are a **professional** — doctor, engineer, lawyer, CA, or architect
- You are a **pensioner receiving Rs.10,000/month or more** (except widows, differently-abled, or ex-servicemen)
- You are a **former or current MP, MLA, Minister, or Mayor**

**Important:** Even a small mismatch between your Aadhaar name and bank name can pause your installments. Get it correct from day one.

## What Documents Do You Need?

1. **Aadhaar Card** — mandatory, must match bank records exactly
2. **Bank Account Details** — account number + IFSC code
3. **Land Ownership Records** — Khasra / Khatauni / Patta documents
4. **Mobile Number** — linked to Aadhaar for OTP verification
5. **Passport-size photograph**
6. **Caste Certificate** — if applying under SC/ST category
7. **Resident/Domicile Proof** — if required in your state

Keep originals and self-attested photocopies ready. Most states now accept digital documents in 2025.

## How to Apply — Step by Step

### Online (Fastest Route)

1. Visit **pmkisan.gov.in**
2. Click **"New Farmer Registration"** on the homepage
3. Select **Rural or Urban Farmer** — choose correctly
4. Enter your **Aadhaar number + mobile number + State**
5. Fill in **land details** from your Record of Rights (RoR)
6. Enter **bank account details** carefully — one wrong digit = no money
7. Upload all required documents
8. Submit and save your **Application Reference Number**

**Pro Tip:** Use Chrome on desktop for a smoother experience. Mobile browsers sometimes glitch during the document upload step.

### Offline (Visit CSC / Block Office)

1. Visit your nearest **Common Service Centre (CSC)** or **Gram Panchayat office**
2. Carry all original documents
3. The CSC operator registers you on the portal on your behalf
4. Collect an **acknowledgment slip** — this is your proof of registration

### Common Mistakes to Avoid:
- **Mismatched Aadhaar name** in bank account — installment gets blocked
- **Joint account number** entered instead of individual account — rejected
- **Skipping e-KYC** — mandatory since 2023, still blocking thousands in 2025

## Zero-Gravity FAQs

**Q1: Can I check if my name is in the beneficiary list?**
Yes! Go to pmkisan.gov.in, click Beneficiary Status, and enter your Aadhaar number, account number, or mobile number. Your full payment history and current status appear instantly.

**Q2: My installment is stuck. What should I do?**
Usually it is an e-KYC issue or a name mismatch. Visit your nearest CSC or call the **PM Kisan Helpline: 155261 or 011-24300606.** Most issues are resolved within 7–10 working days.

**Q3: I recently bought land. Can I apply immediately?**
Yes — as long as the land records are registered in your name in the government's revenue records for the current year, you are eligible to apply right away.

**Q4: Is this scheme only for BPL (Below Poverty Line) families?**
No. There is **no income cap** for farmers — as long as you do not fall into the exclusion categories listed above. Even a middle-income farmer with less than 2 hectares fully qualifies.

**Q5: I registered but never received any money. What happened?**
Do not panic. Most cases involve pending e-KYC or bank verification. Login to the portal, check your status dashboard, and complete any flagged steps. The money is waiting — it just needs your clearance to fly.
